About us

CropRocket is a platform which aims at making the process of farming easy. Everyone, right from amateurs to professionals can benefit from this website. It will help in increasing yield and profit including financial suggestions. On providing region, sub-region and soil type, CropRocket will use these parameters to predict the most suitable crop. Farmers will receive information regarding various banks and loan policies. CropRocket also aims on tackling a common issue faced by the farmers that is, pest attacks. Crops worth almost Rs.50,000 crore are lost every year owing to pest attacks and diseases. Hence, farmers will also get information regarding the most probable pest attacks and the required pesticides. Suggestions regarding fertilizers will also be provided. CropRocket has acknowledged the powerful role that farmers play in everybody's lives and will only work to make their job easier.
